Before i begin to do anything i need to decide what type of robot it will be, and after thinking it over i set on a 
four-wheel-drive eggbeater.

Before i started to work on the design i needed to decide on the components and parts that im going to use.


For the drive motors i decided to go with the Repeat robotics (Mini Brushless 1106 Mk4 - Antweight planetary gearmotor) these
motors have realy good power, speed, and only weight 24.7g.

https://repeat-robotics.com/collections/drive-motors/products/repeat-mini-mk4


For the weapon motor i decided to use (iFlight XING-E Pro 2306 2450KV brushless motor) for this robot i wanted its weapon to spin realy fast
so this motor was perfect and it also has realy good power.

https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005008310935904.html?spm=a2g0o.productlist.0.0.11bak3q0k3q0UI&mp=1&pdp_npi=6%40dis%21EUR%21EUR+38.99%21EUR+38.99%21%21EUR+38.99%21%21%21%400b0fe32e17815412350734105e0ecc%2112000044574807097%21ct%21LV%214900721650%21%211%210%21#nav-review


For the battery, i decided on (Turnigy BoltX LiHV 4S 450mAh 15.2V 80C LiPo Battery)

https://hobbyking.com/en_us/450mah-4s-15-2v-lihv.html

https://repeat-robotics.com/collections/antweight/products/repeat-am32-dual-brushless-drive-esc
